● Question types you can ask

  Clones (similar players)
  - Find a clone of Harry Kane in La Liga
  - Who plays like Rodri
  - Players similar to Lamine Yamal under 23

  Budget clones
  - A striker like Haaland under €40M
  - Someone like Bruno Fernandes for less than 50 million

  Archetype (playing style)
  - What kind of player is Rodri
  - What's Saliba's statistical style
  - Describe Pedri's archetype

  Value (market estimate)
  - Show me undervalued midfielders
  - Is Gvardiol overvalued
  - Most underpriced forwards in Serie A

  Intel (live news/injury/rumours)
  - Any injury news on Saka
  - What's the latest on Mbappé
  - Is Rodri suspended

  Counter (stop an attacker)
  - Find me a defender that can stop Haaland
  - Best centre-back to nullify Kane
  - Who can mark Vinícius in La Liga

  Discover — traits (per-90 style)
  - A ball-carrying midfielder with progressive passes
  - Clinical poachers under 23
  - Aerially dominant centre-backs in Serie A

  Discover — leaderboards (season totals)
  - Who has the most assists in the Premier League
  - Top scorers in Serie A this season
  - Most progressive passes overall

  Compare (head-to-head)
  - Compare Rodri and Bellingham
  - Is Saliba better than Gvardiol aerially
  - Mbappé vs Vinícius

  General (tactics / rules / trivia)
  - What formation suits a high-pressing team
  - Who won the 2022 World Cup
  - Explain the false nine role
